# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Business owners, nonprofit leads, education teams, health-care operators, and public-sector stakeholders evaluating Netpro as a digital technology partner. They arrive with a project, redesign, or growth need and want confidence that the team can consult, design, build, and support serious work.

## Product Purpose

Netpro's site presents the company as a calm, capable technology consultancy with a clear delivery range: web, mobile, design, marketing, SEO, and long-term support. Success means visitors quickly understand the offer, trust the craft, and move toward a consultation.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, reassuring. The interface should feel dark, focused, and premium without becoming crowded or theatrical.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, oversized CTA buttons, crowded card stacks, bright gimmick palettes, glassmorphism for decoration, and AI-looking section templates. Avoid copy that leans on vague hype instead of clear services and proof.

## Design Principles

Show technical confidence through spacing, sharp hierarchy, and restrained motion.

Keep every section scannable: one clear heading, one short explanation, and focused actions.

Use dark surfaces with enough contrast for long reading comfort.

Let blue carry action and identity, while neutral chrome textures create depth.

Reuse tokens and section primitives so later pages feel like one system.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Preserve visible focus states, 44px interactive hit areas where layout allows, reduced-motion fallbacks, readable line lengths, and semantic landmarks.
